HOUSE ORGANIZATION


The House organization is introduced in 24th March 1924. Three Houses are Lythe, Creedy and Hornby. The Fourth House, Scowcroft is introduced in 17th Jan 1949.Then Thambiah house was introduced as Fifth house. These houses named after the Past principals.
